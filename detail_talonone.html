Talon.One

Talon.One is a new venture creating a software for customer loyalty and retention marketing. Its experienced team made the decision of joining them very easy.

Project Details

I joined the company as their 5th developer and together we brought the application from MVP to production, winning several investors during the process. Since we had issues finding an UX designer, the user interface and user flow has been designed this time by myself, applying user research techniques and testing as I learnt from previous designers and from the online Interaction Design course from the University of California. The Frontend technologies used in this project are React, Redux, Webpack and SCSS, in the Backend, mostly Go.
