PetFlow

PetFlow is a project for the course Methodology of Interaction Design. We applied user research methodology and ideation techniques to serve a community of users which is in this case the pet owners.

Project Details

Working in a team of 3, we performed extensive user research such as observations and creating personas. We performed ideation with brainstorming and creative workshops. For the chosen pet owner community, the lack of information and the disparsity of sources that contradict each other led us to create an information and knowledge exchange platform similar to StackOverflow which is oriented to programmers, but we adapted it for the pet owners and the professionals around animals such as veterinaries, pet services and experts in pet laws. For the prototyping, we started with paper prototypes and conducting tests on participants. Later on we created low fi prototypes and finally refined them with Sketch and invisionApp to create a click dummy and showcase the entire user journey.
